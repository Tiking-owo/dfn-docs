# Tofu Nao Workshop

Welcome to the **Fursuit-specific Smart Fan** designed for fursuit enthusiasts. This guide will help you quickly start your cooling experience.

## Ready to Use Out of the Box

1.  **Install the Battery**: Simply insert the included 18650 lithium-ion battery module.
2.  **Power On**: Flip the switch downward. The screen will light up, and the fan will start up with a self-test.
3.  **Basic Use**: After powering on, press the **Left Button** or **Right Button** to select functions.

## Core Functions Overview

*   **Replace Battery**: The battery compartment features a quick-draw design, allowing for tool-free, instant battery replacement. Paired with a spare battery, this enables "unlimited runtime."
*   **Adjust Wind Speed**: In the wind speed interface, press the **Left Button (Decrease)** or **Right Button (Increase)** to directly adjust the wind speed level.
*   **Access Menu**: In the menu interface, press the **Left Button** or **Right Button** to select functions for more settings.

Put on your fursuit head and enjoy the cooling and convenience tailored for you!
